Our Logistics Services

Comprehensive solutions tailored to your specific business needs in Zimbabwe and beyond.

Customs Clearance

Navigating Zimbabwe's customs procedures can be daunting. Our expert agents handle all documentation and clearance at all major border posts including Beitbridge, Forbes (Mutare), Plumtree, and Chirundu.

  • Commercial Cargo Clearing
  • Vehicle Import Clearance
  • Temporary Import Permits (TIP)

ZIMRA Compliance

Stay on the right side of the law with our ZIMRA compliance consulting. we help you understand tariff headings, duty calculations, and tax obligations to avoid costly penalties.

  • Tariff Classification
  • Duty Drawback Claims
  • Compliance Audits

Real-Time Shipment Tracking

Transparency is key to our service. Our advanced tracking platform allows you to monitor your cargo's journey in real-time, from the moment it leaves the supplier to final delivery.

  • GPS Satellite Tracking
  • 24/7 Status Updates
  • Estimated Time of Arrival (ETA)
