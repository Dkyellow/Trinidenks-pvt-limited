Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required for clearing commercial goods?

Generally, you need a Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading or Airway Bill, and any relevant import permits or licenses required for specific goods.

How long does the customs clearance process take at Beitbridge?

With correct documentation, standard clearance takes between 24 to 48 hours. However, we strive to expedite this through pre-clearance procedures.

Do you offer door-to-door delivery within Zimbabwe?

Yes, we provide comprehensive last-mile delivery services from any port of entry to your specific warehouse or business location across the country.

What are your operating hours?

Our main offices are open Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM. However, our border agents operating at major ports often work extended hours to match port operations.
